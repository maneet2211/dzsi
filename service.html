Professional Services

Professional services offerings are designed to help our customers rapidly deploy, scale and optimize their broadband networks for agility and reliability. These offerings can accelerate the turn-up of new services, as well as provide product and technology knowledge transfer.

DZS professional services projects can range from equipment provisioning in a central office or remote cabinet, up to a complete onsite network roll out with professional project management.

Learn more about DZS professional services

Architecture & Designs

Customers benefit from DZS Architecture & Design experts with comprehensive and multi-technological experience (GPON, VDSL, IP, FiberLAN and legacy technologies: TDM, SDH, PDH).

Our team can assist customers in the design, creation and operation of their network infrastructure..

  • Analysis of the requirements
  • Re-design and minimizing downtime (service interruption)
  • Optimization and migration scenarios
  • Security, power consumption, Green IT
